Chon main, chuong trinh se tu sinh khoa de o thu muc: "C:/keys/private.key". Van ban de ma hoa da duoc khai bao san: "Text to be encrypted". Ban ma va ban giai ma se duoc in ra

5. SHA512. Bam SHA512. Sinh ra ban bam do dai 512 bit cho moi van ban dau vao.

6. SignatureTest. Sinh ra chu ky RSA voi thuat toan bam MD5.
